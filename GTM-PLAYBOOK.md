## Appendix: Finding HR Contacts

### Method 1: LinkedIn Search (Free)

For each target FQHC, search LinkedIn for:
- "HR Director" + "[FQHC Name]"
- "Talent Acquisition" + "[FQHC Name]"
- "Human Resources Manager" + "[FQHC Name]"
- "Chief People Officer" + "[FQHC Name]"

### Method 2: FQHC Careers Pages (Free)

Most FQHC careers pages list a contact email (usually hr@domain or careers@domain). Check:
- Their careers page (URLs listed in Tier 1-4 above)
- Their "Contact Us" page
- Job posting details (often include application email)

### Method 3: Apollo.io ($49/mo)

Apollo.io has verified emails for most organizations over 50 employees. Search by:
- Company name ‚Üí filter by title "HR" or "Talent" or "Recruiting"
- Get direct email + phone number
- Export as CSV for outreach

### Method 4: Hunter.io (Free/Paid)

- Enter the FQHC domain (e.g., altamed.org)
- Shows email pattern + known emails
- Free for 25 lookups/month

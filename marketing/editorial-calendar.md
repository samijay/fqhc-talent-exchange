# FQHC Talent Exchange — Editorial Calendar

## Content Philosophy
Mission-driven strategic knowledge base. Every article gives FQHC professionals — from CHWs to CFOs — actionable intelligence they cannot get anywhere else. Real case studies, real billing codes, real OKRs, real downloadable tools. No SEO slop. No listicles. If a CPCA executive reads it and thinks "I need to share this with my board," we did our job.

## Publishing Cadence
- **1 long-form strategic article per week** (2,000-3,500 words, publish Tuesday)
- **1 downloadable resource per article** (Excel toolkit, OKR tracker, checklist, framework)
- **2-3 companion posts** to drive article traffic (hook, data point, question)
- **1 LinkedIn Newsletter issue per week** (repurpose article, publish Wednesday)

## Content Pillars
1. **Revenue Transformation** — 340B, ECM, DPC, co-visit billing, RCM AI, IPA models
2. **Operational Efficiency** — Team-based care, scope-of-practice, AI documentation, telehealth
3. **Compliance & Risk** — SB 525, 340B audits, billing compliance, HRSA OSV prep
4. **Policy Intelligence** — H.R. 1 impacts, CalAIM, PPS changes, legislative tracking
5. **Workforce Strategy** — Hiring, retention, career development, cultural humility
6. **Strategic Foresight** — Resilience scoring, regional analysis, M&A trends, AI adoption

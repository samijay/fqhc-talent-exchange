## 6. Governance & Leadership

- **51%+ patient board members** (Section 330)
- Representative of served population (race, ethnicity, sex)

## 7. Strategic Planning Frameworks

### HRSA Requirement
HRSA requires FQHCs to adopt a strategic plan **at least every 3 years** but does not prescribe an approach.

## How to Use This Bibliography

**For FQHC CEOs/COOs:** Start with Sections 6 (Governance), 7 (Strategy), and 5 (Financial Management). The NACHC C-Suite programs and Commonwealth Fund surveys are your benchmarking foundation.

**For policy staff:** Sections 3 (Undocumented Care) and 11 (Government Resources) are your evidence base. The Cervantes dialysis study and Zallman contributions data are the most-cited in Congressional testimony.

**For HR/workforce leaders:** The Commonwealth Fund 2024 survey (77% mental health shortages, 70% physician shortages) frames the recruitment challenge. The FQHC Talent Exchange at [fqhctalent.com](https://www.fqhctalent.com) tracks salary data, layoffs, and workforce intelligence for California.

**For board members:** The Geiger Gibson Program and CHC Chronicles provide mission-grounding. NACHC board training and the compliance manual chapters (19, 20) are governance requirements.

**For researchers:** The HRSA UDS data warehouse, NACHC Chartbook, and Geiger Gibson program are your primary data sources. Sara Rosenbaum's body of work is the intellectual foundation.
